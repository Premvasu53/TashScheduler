About Task Scheduler

TaskScheduler helps you manage your tasks efficiently by providing features such as creating, editing, and deleting tasks.

Follow the steps below to create a task:

  1. Click on the "Create Task" button.
  2. Fill in the details for your task, including the title, date, and description.
  3. Click "Add Task" to add the task to your list.
